# Dici Beauty Supply — Signature

**Concept:** "The Counter." Dici reframed as a neighborhood *apothecary-style beauty
supply* on Main Street, City of Orange NJ. Grounded in heritage cosmetics houses
(Kiehl's, Apothecary 87): warm kraft-paper backgrounds, ingredient-forward honesty,
a counter that still answers questions.

## Locked divergence lane (honored)
- **Accent:** #6F4E37 roasted mocha (token `--mocha`), with terracotta-rose + dried-sage + aged-brass supports.
- **Display font:** Boska (loaded from Fontshare), set in italic for the editorial "voice" lines.
- **Layout archetype:** stacked horizontal bands, alternating image/text, with one deep-mocha band inverted.
- **Era / mood:** artisanal — hand-stocked, letterpress kickers, wax-seal motifs, kraft grain.
- **Imagery:** Pexels cosmetics/skincare/tools, warm-toned set chosen to match the mocha lane; every image used exactly once.

## The signature moment
The **Apothecary Aisle Index** (`components/Index.tsx`): a numbered index-card list of
four shelves. Hovering or tapping a row slides the aisle name, expands a hand-written
note, and cross-fades the live product "plate" beside it, with a slow warm **shelf-light
sweep** raking across the glass (the same `.shelf-light` signature appears on the hero
product plate). It reads like the index taped behind a heritage counter, brought to life.

Secondary touches: a slow rotating wax-seal stamp ("est · orange"), drifting product
chips, a running apothecary marquee in mocha, and a live "open now" pill computed from the
verified hours table.
